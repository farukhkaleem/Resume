About Amina

Professional Summary

Amina Malik is a dedicated couple therapist and life coach with extensive experience in relationship counseling, personal development, and emotional wellness. She specializes in helping individuals and couples not only strengthen their relationships with others, but also cultivate a healthier, more fulfilling relationship with themselves. With a compassionate and holistic approach, Amina supports clients dealing with challenges such as depression, anxiety, and emotional stress, guiding them toward clarity, balance, and resilience. By integrating traditional wisdom, modern psychology, and spiritual insight, she empowers her clients to build stronger, healthier, and more balanced lives.

Approach & Background

With a holistic approach to therapy, Amina combines traditional wisdom, modern psychological understanding, and spiritual insight with evidence-based techniques. She believes that true healing comes from addressing the mind, heart, and spirit together. With a strong background in relationship coaching and years of practical experience in understanding human psychology, Amina creates safe and compassionate spaces where individuals and couples can find growth, clarity, and emotional balance.

Professional Experience & Focus

Relationship with Others: Helping couples and families improve communication, rebuild trust, and strengthen emotional bonds.
Relationship with Self: Guiding individuals toward greater self-awareness, confidence, and inner peace.
Emotional Wellness: Supporting clients in managing depression, anxiety, and stress through holistic and compassionate strategies.
Premarital Counseling: Preparing individuals and couples for marriage by fostering emotional maturity, spiritual alignment, and realistic expectations.
Parenting Support: Helping parents navigate challenges, strengthen their bond with children, and create nurturing family environments.
Personal Growth: Empowering clients to discover purpose, cultivate resilience, and live more balanced, fulfilling lives.
